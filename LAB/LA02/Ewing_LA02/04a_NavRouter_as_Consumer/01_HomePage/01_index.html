A Sample Home Page !

This layout has a media query that makes the web page "responsive" meaning that it looks good regardless of the width of the viewport, whether desktop or mobile device.

Here's how it works: narrow your browser window until you see a change in the layout. The nav bar should go under the title and the top margin of the content area gets taller so that the top of the content is not hidden under the taller titleNav.
